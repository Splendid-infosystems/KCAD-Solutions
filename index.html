Introduction to CAD/CAM and CAE

With the advancement in the technology, Automobile industry is striving for delivering products of increasing variety, user friendly and higher quality in the context of increasing global competition. Industries cannot survive worldwide competition unless they introduce new products with better quality, at lower costs and with shorter lead-time. With dramatic changes in computing power and wider availability of software tools for design and production, engineers are now using Computer Aided Design (CAD for Design), Computer Aided Manufacturing (CAM - for Manufacturing) and Computer Aided Engineering (CAE - for Analysis & Simulation) systems to automate their design and production processes. These technologies are now used every day for sorts of different engineering tasks with the objective to save time and increase production.

Our unique courses are specially designed considering current industry demands and job requirement, which not only help engineers to secure a job in the industry but also it will help them to boost their design career by acquiring vast practical knowledge at the start of career.

Our training content is aligned with current industry work that gives extra edge to students while attaining interviews as well as working on independently on projects.
